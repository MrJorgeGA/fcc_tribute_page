Manuel Sadosky

Father of the Computer Science in Argentina

A time line of Manuel Sadosky's life:

  • 1914 - Sadosky is born April 13th in Buenos Aires
  • 1940 - Completes his PhD. in Physics and Mathematics at the University of Buenos Aires (UBA)
  • 1949 - After 3 years as researcher in Europe, comes back to Argentina
  • 1955 - Took uo a position as professor at the Faculty of Exact and Natural Sciences (FCEN) of the UBA
  • 1957 - He's elected as vice-dean of FCEN
  • 1961 - Alongside Rebeca Guber and a group of mathematicians, Clementina, a Ferranti Mercury computer, is build and the Computational Institute is founded
  • 1966 - After the UBA is intervened by the military junta installed that year, Sadosky resign all his positions at the university
  • 1974 - Political persecution pushes him into exile, first in Venezuela, later in Spain
